1. Ardha Halasana

Lie on your back and slowly raise your legs to a 90-degree angle. Breathe deeply while holding the position. Helps improve blood circulation and core strength.

2. Savasana

Lie flat on your back with your arms relaxed at your sides. Focus on your breathing to relax the body and mind completely.

3. Ardha Sirsana

Begin in a kneeling position and slowly raise your hips while supporting your head with your hands. This pose strengthens the upper body and improves balance.

4. Ardha Sarvangasana

Lie on your back and lift your hips and legs, supporting your lower back with your hands. Focus on keeping the body aligned and breathing deeply.

5. Paschimattanasana

Sit with your legs stretched out and bend forward to touch your toes. This pose stretches the spine and hamstrings, improving flexibility.

6. Balasana

Sit back on your heels and stretch your arms forward while lowering your chest. This pose relieves stress and improves spinal flexibility.
